### 1. Negentropy as the Measure of Life

Schrödinger argued living systems survive by feeding on **negentropy**. This paper maps this directly to software architecture — a healthy system acts as a negentropy pump, ingesting chaotic data and producing structured output.

### 2. Polyvagal Theory → Distributed Systems

A revolutionary mapping:

- **Ventral Vagal** (safe) = Steady state, 200 OK, healthy autoscaling
- **Sympathetic** (fight/flight) = Incident response, retry storms, PagerDuty
- **Dorsal Vagal** (freeze) = System collapse, circuit breakers open, deadlock

### 3. The Handmade/Rust Synthesis

Resolution of the tension between:

- **NPM/Anxious** = Hyperdependent, fragile
- **Handmade/Avoidant** = Zero-dependency, isolated
- **Rust/Secure** = Verified dependencies, explicit contracts
