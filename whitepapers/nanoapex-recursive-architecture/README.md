## The Other Side of the Coin

Traditional agents execute pre-defined goals. The NanoApex architecture inverts this: **agents must be capable of recursively questioning and discovering their own goals**. This is Level 6 Meta-Awareness — not just thinking, but thinking about thinking, and deciding what to think about.

## Abstract

NanoApex is a Level 6 Meta-Aware AGI architecture implementing **Recursive IntroSpEction (RISE)** — a protocol enabling agents to discover new goals through self-reflection rather than pure optimization of given objectives. The architecture integrates the Mixture of Inversion Experts (MoIE) for alignment, Wan 2.2 for visual imagination, and addresses frontier scientific hypotheses with rigor.
